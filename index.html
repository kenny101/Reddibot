Reddibot

Fetch images from any of your favorite subreddits. Limitless image generation!








Command Prefix: send
To show the command's menu:
send help
Send a random image from a Subreddit:
send me <name of a subreddit>
Subscribe to receive new images from a subreddit:
send more <name of a subreddit>
Stop receiving images from a subreddit:
send less <name of a subreddit>
